Mission de la Casa is using the Shift Medical platform to make it easier for staff members to pick up open shifts.

ATTENTION! A $50 bonus is offered for every shift you pick up using the new platform.

How does the platform work?

 

Three Simple Steps:

1. Staff members are notified via text and e-mail when a new shift is open.

2. If you are interested in the shift, use the link included in the text and e-mail to login and apply.

3. The staff member who is selected for the shift is immediately notified via text and e-mail.
